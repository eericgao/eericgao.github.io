About us

Tucked away in a sunlit studio in Red Hook, Brooklyn, Kiln Ceramics is a small-batch pottery studio crafting rustic, hand-thrown ceramics for those who appreciate artistry in the everyday. Inspired by the industrial charm and creative energy of our neighborhood, our work blends traditional craftsmanship with modern simplicity, resulting in pieces that are both timeless and functional. Each mug, bowl, and plate is carefully shaped, glazed, and fired with subtle imperfections that make every item unique. Whether you’re sipping espresso from a perfectly weighted cup or setting the table with earthy stoneware, our ceramics are made to be used, loved, and passed down.

Beyond cups and bowls, we create plates, serving dishes, vases, and specialty pieces, all designed with tactile textures and organic glazes that feel as good in your hands as they look on your table. Our studio embraces slow, intentional craftsmanship, using sustainable materials and firing methods that honor the process of handmade work. Rooted in the underground creative culture of Brooklyn, Kiln Ceramics is more than a brand—it’s a philosophy of living with objects that tell a story.
